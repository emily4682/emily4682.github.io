Autoamtic Payslip Generator

Language/s Used:

UiPath (based in VB)

Description:

This project was developed as part of my work experience with Serco. I built this in UiPath which is a piece of software used for RPA automations.

This bot will read in data from an excel file and perform calculations and formatting on that data to produce and eventually email a payslip to each employee on the list.

This process can currently process 3 types of payslips - UK, USA & Belgian Payslips. The bot will calculate all taxes, total income (holiday, sick leave etc..) and any other outgoing payments such as student loan repayments.

Once calculated, the data is formatted into a PDF and that PDF is sent out to the employee's email address.
